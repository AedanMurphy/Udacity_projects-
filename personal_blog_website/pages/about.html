My Origin Story

So you want to know the behind the scenes?

I used to live in Vietnam, and when I came back to Australia I went through a scary process of establishing myself back into my old life. I was a bit lost and bit lonely. One day, I worked past a bouldering gym and found it interestin but didn't think beyond that.

That night however, I came across a video of Alex Honnold and his free solo of El'Captian. I was hooked! The thought of absolutely losing yourself to a physical climbing problem sounded like a relief. Within that week, I turned up to a gym and started climbing.

That year COVID hit, and our lives came to a frozen stand still. This, did not waiver my interest in climbing but only strenghened it. Now...all I want to do is share what I learnt and hopefully have a few readers write me back and tell me about their journeys.
